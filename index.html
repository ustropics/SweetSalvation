Phase 0: Where will you start?

In the early stages of this project my focus was to conduct research about the product, service, and industry.

The home-based baking business will corelate with regulations, laws, and planning which will require multiple decisions about the product focus, business plan, licenses and permits, business entity, marketing plan, separate equipment, packaging and labels, and baking goods (Duermyer, 2020).

Covid-19 pandemic has boosted the demand for baked goods and other consumer staples as social distancing has become prominent during this challenging time. Statement from Blog.Technavio, “Buyers are increasing their consumption of comfort foods during this time, making bakery items a popular choice for snacks, desserts, or even meals (Blog.technavio.org, 2020).”

Phase 1: Opportunity Identification Activities

The baking industry is consumed with sugary sweets and the opportunity is homemade nutritional desserts that have the same great flavors and textures as their sugary counterparts.

The target will be consumers who enjoy nutritional foods like fruits and vegetables are more likely to consume or be intrigued to try healthy desserts and will most likely be a supporter.

Phase 2: What Is? Activities

Significant trends in the baking industry for pastries and chocolates have been rising to the top in recent years.

Most people want to enjoy a sweet healthy dessert without compromising the balance of perceived tastes, while also giving new experiences.

Textures like crunchy are growing in importance as consumers search for a unique product that is visually appealing and tasteful. “In fact, 62% want to try new textures (Evoniuk, n.d.).”

The desserts must be healthy without compromising the flavor, texture, or visual appearance. People are drawn to colorful and decorative products especially food that is being consumed. The colorful and elegant design coupled with taste will intrigue people of all ages.

Phase 3: What If? Activities

The brainstormed ideas for a home-made baking business have key attributes that require attention to detail, accuracy, a high-risk environment, time pressure, and potential distractions.

Phase 4: What Wows? Activities

The three concept ideas theMuffookie, the Mufficake, and the Brookie provide a variance in texture and experience.

The global bakery market is shifting to adapt to the high demand including legumes, oats, probiotics, fortified margarine, and cereals. “The global bakery product market size was estimated at USD 203.8 billion in 2018 and is projected to expand at a CAGR of 3.2% from 2019 to 2025 (Grand View Research, 2019).”

Phase 5: What Works? Activities

The product was only introduced to gauge the interest of three unique combinations. It was not tested through real people or any finished products.

Not many aspects of the learning launch failed except for the hesitation to try a healthier option of familiar sweet dessert treats. The concept ideas presented were intriguing to consumers and they wanted more than just a picture.

The global market supports new bakeries as it continues to increase in size and with technological advancements the goal will be to implement an e-commerce online ordering and delivery service to accommodate to the new economic growth.

Consumers will be engaged through free social media platforms, farmers markets, and word of mouth.
